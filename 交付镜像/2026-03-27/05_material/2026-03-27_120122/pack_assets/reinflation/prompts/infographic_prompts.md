# Reinflation infographic prompts

## Anchor: oil_price_inflation_expectation
Create a clean financial infographic in Chinese, editorial style, dark-on-light, showing the transmission chain: geopolitical shock -> oil price -> inflation expectations -> longer-for-higher rates -> asset repricing. Include icons for oil barrel, inflation gauge, Fed building, bond yield curve, US equities. Avoid stock-photo collage and avoid heavy gradients.

## Anchor: fed_stagflation_signal
Create a compact information graphic summarizing three official signals: FOMC uncertainty increased, SEP inflation median above 2%, Powell warning on higher inflation and slower growth. Use a three-column structure with strong hierarchy, restrained red/amber accents, no cartoon style.

## Anchor: asset_repricing_map
Create a decision-map infographic in Chinese for investors: bonds, growth stocks, commodities, gold. Show which assets are pressured or supported when re-inflation risk rises. Use arrows, legend, and simple institutional layout.
